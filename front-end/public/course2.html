For the course, The main goal is to improve advanced reading comprehension, vocabulary, and discussion/presentation abilities. A variety of themes will be covered through reading, speaking, listening, and writing activities. Through exposure to varied speech/writing styles, structural patterns, advanced vocabulary, and phrases, including Korean proverbs and idioms, students will get a deeper understanding of Korean language and culture. The training emphasizes interpersonal, interpretative, and presentational communication abilities.
