This Standard Mandarin Chinese course is ideal for beginners with little or no prior knowledge. This course uses a communicative method to teach students essential Mandarin Chinese skills such as listening, speaking, reading, and writing. This course emphasizes language elements and incorporates traditional and modern cultural material. This course focuses on: (a) establishing a strong foundation in pronunciation, grammar, characters, reading, and writing; and (b) developing vocabulary and sentence patterns in communicative contexts. The goal of this course is to help students learn information in a communicative and cultural context, utilizing interpersonal, interpretative, and presentational communication skills.
